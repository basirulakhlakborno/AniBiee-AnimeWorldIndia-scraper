# Palette's UX Journal

## 2026-02-04 - [Navigation Clarity & Accessibility]
**Learning:** Generic links (like "GitHub") that don't point to the specific resource can frustrate users. Always ensure external links are specific and safe.
**Action:** Audit external links for specificity and add `rel="noopener noreferrer"` and accessible labels/attributes (like `aria-hidden`) by default.
