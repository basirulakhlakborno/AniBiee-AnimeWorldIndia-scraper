## 2025-05-15 - Empty State Dead Ends
**Learning:** The application's error pages (404, 403) were implemented as static visual displays without navigation, creating a dead-end for users. This likely stemmed from treating them as "screens" rather than "functional pages".
**Action:** Always verify that "empty state" or error pages contain at least one clear call-to-action (like "Back to Home") to maintain user flow.
