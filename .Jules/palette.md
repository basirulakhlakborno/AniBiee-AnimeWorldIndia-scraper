## 2024-05-24 - Dead-end Error Pages
**Learning:** Error pages (like 404 and 403) without clear navigation options create dead ends that frustrate users, especially in single-page apps or standalone sites where browser navigation isn't obvious. Providing an explicit "Return to Homepage" button improves recovery flow significantly.
**Action:** Always include a prominent "Return to Homepage" or "Go Back" action button on full-page error states.
