## 2024-05-23 - Semantic Structure & Interactive States
**Learning:** Even simple landing pages suffer significantly without semantic landmarks (like `<main>`) and clear interactive states. Users relying on keyboards or screen readers can easily get lost or miss actionable elements.
**Action:** Always check for `<main>` wrappers on landing pages and ensure every interactive element (buttons, links) has distinct `:hover` and `:focus-visible` styles.
